Definición

$\forall a,d \in \mathbb{Z} $, $ d \mid a \Longleftrightarrow a = d\cdot k, k \in \mathbb{Z}$

Propiedades

1\. $$d \mid a \land d \mid b \Rightarrow d \mid a + b$$

<LogicFlow>
- $$\Rightarrow a = k_1d \land b = k_2d $$
- $$\Rightarrow a+b = k_1 d + k_2 d$$
- $$\Rightarrow a+b = (k_1 + k_2) d$$
- $$d \mid a + b$$
</LogicFlow>

Observación: no sé cumple un sí sollo sí 
<LogicFlow>
- $$ d \mid a+b \land (d\nmid a \lor d\nmid b) $$
- $$ 6 \mid 3+3 \land 6\nmid 3 $$
</LogicFlow>

2\. $$d \mid a \Longleftrightarrow d \mid ac, \forall c\in \Z$$
<LogicFlow>
- $$ a = dk$$
- $$ \Leftrightarrow ac = dkc \land kc\in \Z$$
- $$ \Leftrightarrow ac = dk'$$
- $$ \Leftrightarrow ac = dk'$$
- $$ \Leftrightarrow d \mid ac$$
</LogicFlow>

3\. $$d \mid a \Longleftrightarrow d \mid -a \Longleftrightarrow d \mid abs(a)$$
<LogicFlow>
- $$ d \mid a$$
- $$ \Leftrightarrow a = dk $$
- $$ \Leftrightarrow -a = d(-k), (-k)=k'\in \Z $$
- $$ \Leftrightarrow -a = dk' $$
- $$ \Leftrightarrow d \mid -a $$
</LogicFlow>
Luego, $$d \mid a \land d \mid -a \Longleftrightarrow d\mid abs(a)$$
